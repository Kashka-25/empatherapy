EER — Emotional Emergency Relief

Short-Form, Faster-Response Support

EER exists for moments when emotional support is needed quickly, but a full-length session or standard pricing isn't accessible.

Sessions are shorter, more focused, and offered with increased response priority for subscribers.

EER is not a crisis service or emergency care, but a compassionate bridge during moments of emotional overwhelm.

EER Subscription Options

Subscriptions make EER more accessible, more affordable, and allow Empatherapy to reserve capacity for those who may need support at short notice.

EER Support Subscription

$111 AUD/month

Includes:

  • 2 EER sessions per month (30 minutes each)
  • Reduced session cost compared to one-off EER
  • Priority response & scheduling
  • Reserved monthly capacity

Ideal for: Those navigating instability, transition, or recurring emotional overwhelm who want reassurance that support is available when needed.

Unused sessions do not roll over, but the presence of support remains.

One-Off EER Session

For those unable to commit to a subscription, EER sessions may be booked individually when availability allows.

10% of all EER sessions contribute to the Empatherapy Community Healing Fund

EER & The Healing Fund

10% of all EER sessions contribute to the Empatherapy Healing Fund. Healing Fund resources may be used to partially or fully subsidize EER sessions for those in hardship.

Clients and community members may also support this work through Healing Fund Subscriptions, which directly deposit funds into the Healing Fund on a recurring basis.

A Note on Safety

If you are at risk of harming yourself or others, or need immediate help, please contact local emergency services or a crisis line.

EER is not equipped for emergency intervention.

How to Request EER Support

To request EER support, please email hello@empatherapy.co with the subject line "EER Request"

Please Include:

  • Your name (first name is enough)
  • Whether you're currently an EER subscriber
  • Brief description of what you need support with (a few words or a sentence is enough)
  • How urgent this feels for you:
    • I need support as soon as possible
    • Within the next few days
    • I'm okay to wait if needed
  • Anything important we should know before we meet (optional)

You will never be asked to justify your need for support. This is an invitation, not an assessment.

Response time: Subscribers receive priority response. We aim to respond to all EER requests as quickly as possible, though same-day responses are not guaranteed.
