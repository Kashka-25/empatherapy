Support before or after psychedelic experiences

This offering supports meaning-making, emotional integration, grounding, and reflection around psychedelic experiences. This is not facilitation or medical therapy, but integrative mentoring.

Psychedelic experiences can be profound, overwhelming, beautiful, and destabilizing all at once. Integration is the bridge between the journey and your everyday life‚Äîa space to make sense of what surfaced, to honor what was revealed, and to ground the insights into meaningful change.

Whether you're processing a recent journey or working with experiences from months or years ago, integration creates a container for:

  • Making meaning of symbolic, emotional, or spiritual content
  • Processing difficult or challenging experiences
  • Translating insights into practical life changes
  • Navigating shifts in identity, relationships, or worldview
  • Grounding expanded states back into embodied presence

How Sessions Work

Integration sessions are held as 75-minute conversations via video or phone. We move at your pace, honoring whatever needs to emerge.

Sessions may include:

  • Witnessing and holding space
  • Somatic practices to ground and regulate
  • Meaning-making through metaphor, ritual, or creative reflection
  • Identifying actionable steps to embody insights
  • Processing fear, grief, or confusion

Important Note

I do not provide substances, facilitate journeys, or offer medical advice. This is integration support only.

This isn't therapy, but it is therapeutic. It's a space where your journey is welcomed without judgment, and where integration becomes a conscious, supported process.
