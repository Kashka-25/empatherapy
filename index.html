üíö Empatherapy Community Healing Fund

Healing shouldn't be a privilege. The Empatherapy Community Healing Fund exists to make support accessible to those who need it most.

How it works:

  • 10% of every session fee goes directly into the fund
  • Community members can donate any amount
  • Funds are used to subsidize or fully cover sessions for those experiencing financial hardship
  • All therapists in our directory contribute 10% to the fund

This is care as collective responsibility. When you invest in your healing, you're also investing in someone else's.
