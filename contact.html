Connect with a Specific Therapist

Every practitioner offers a free 10-minute discovery call before you book a full session.

How to Book a Discovery Call:

  1. Browse practitioners on the Meet Your Therapist page
  2. Click "Book Discovery Call" on their practitioner card
  3. Email opens with subject line pre-filled (e.g., "Discovery Call with Cassidy")
  4. Include:
    • Your name
    • Brief note about what you're seeking
    • Your availability (day/time preferences)
  5. They'll respond with available times for your free 10-minute call

Example Email:

To: hello@empatherapy.co
Subject: Discovery Call with Cassidy

Hi, I'm Alex. I'm interested in psychedelic integration support and emotional processing work. I'd love to book a discovery call with Cassidy to see if we're a good fit.

I'm available weekday mornings (9am-12pm AEST) or Saturday afternoons.

Thank you!

For Emotional Emergency Relief (EER)

If you need immediate emotional support through our EER service, please email with the subject line "EER Request"

Include:

  • Your name
  • Whether you're a current EER subscriber
  • Brief description of what you need support with

Important: EER is not a crisis or emergency service. If you are in immediate danger, please contact local emergency services or a crisis line.
