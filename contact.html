For Emotional Emergency Relief (EER)

If you need immediate emotional support through our EER service, please email with the subject line "EER Request" and include:

  • Your name
  • Whether you're a current EER subscriber
  • Brief description of what you need support with
  • How urgent it feels (as soon as possible / within a few days / can wait)

Important: EER is not a crisis or emergency service. If you are in immediate danger, please contact local emergency services or a crisis line.
